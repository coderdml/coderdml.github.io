Promise链式编程

2021-08-01 2 min read 
<script>
    new Promise((resolve,reject)=>{
        setTimeout(()=>{
            // resolve('Hello World')
            reject('err message')
        },1000)
        }).then((data)=>{         //也可以在then中传入两个函数代替catch
            console.log(data)
        }).catch((err)=>{
            console.log(err)
        })
</script>

Promise的all方法

需要获取到两个异步请求的结果，确认两个请求都收到

Promise.all([
    new Promise((resolve)=>{
        setTimeout(()=>{
            resolve({name:'coderdml',age:18})
        },1000)
    }),
    new Promise((resolve)=>{
        setTimeout(()=>{
            resolve({name:'coderwhy',age:19})
        },2000)
    })
]).then((res)=>{
    console.log(res)
})
